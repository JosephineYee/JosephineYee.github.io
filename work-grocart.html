Grocart - A Mobile App for Grocery Shopping

Overview

The grocart mobile app is a UX challenge that I created to practice my thinking and deisgn skills. It is designed to enable consumers to buy groceries in a convenient way.

Role and Responsibility
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Wireframing
  • Prototoyping
  • Visual Design
Tool Used
  • Figma
Scenario

I am a UX designer at a startup company that is making a new mobile grocery delivery app where instacart is a competitor.

Task

Create a user flow for the entire process of creating and submitting a grocery delivery: selecting a nearby store, adding food to the cart, checking out, and choosing a delivery time.

User flow

To anticipate the path that the consumer will take. I designed a user flows for selecting store,a dding producst to cart, checkout, and choosing a delivery time. I also include the process of adding other payment options and purchasing products again.

Wireframe

I usually start my design process with low-fidelity wireframes. This way I can strcuture teh design by placing images, text, button, and icons.

Screens

UI Design

Rationale

I design a grocery mobile app

  • I used green and yellow palette to represent freshness and optimism.
  • I apply drop-down menu in terms of selecting a large list to have a clean layout and easy access to selection.
  • I used a darker color for CTA to attract user.

    • What I have learned from this challenge

    I really enjoyed working on this grocery app. Doing this challege makes me realize that understanding your user's objectives and putting them at the center of the process will help you to analyze the flow better. Although this challenge focused on ideation or user flow, I did research on some competitors to understand and gain a picture of their strategy and process.
